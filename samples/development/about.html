About

So this this the part where you'll want to add some useful information about what you've got going on. This can be an origin story, a brief service overview, or a more of a personal insight into yourself and/or business. Whatever you choose to go with, be sure to make it apparent to your audience what it is that you do and why they should choose you.

People, Profit, Planet

This is optional of course, but sometimes it's good to give the people a little more information. You've no doubt explained what you do and why you're the best choice, but for those still teetering on the edge, sub-sections like this one can give them an extra boost. Tell them something like how you give back to the community, or gave kids a scholarship because of an aid program you head, or provided relief service to a country after a natural disaster. People like seeing stories of how businesses put the human back in humanity, after all.

Initiatives

You may want to do something like communicating projects. Maybe you have a goal you are in the process of achieving (or have already achieved). Perhaps there's a unique way of running your company. There may be special talent that you feel would not only add value to your work process, but would make people want to hire you. Whatever you feel people ought to know about you, your brand, and your process - add it to this page!
