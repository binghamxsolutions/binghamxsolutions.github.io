So what's next?

Well, you have successfully completed the first step - choosing me to help you build your digital solution! Now that that's out of the way, we should probably work on the process of actually building that solution, don't you think?

Generally speaking, the major components of any project are as follows:

  1. Planning
  2. Analysing
  3. Designing
  4. Implementing

But project management steps are supposed to be something that gets integrated into the process itself instead of treating them as a stand-alone tool so… Let's simplify things! Below, you will find quick tips on what is left to do regardless of whether you want a logo design, a web design, or a website (or page!) developed.

Brainstorm!
First things first, we need to get an idea of what you'd like. Logo? Design? Full site? (Maybe all of the above?)
Color!
Next, we should determine what colors you'd prefer to tie everything together. Think of your grade-school color schemes.
Build!
Finally! After everything else has been finalized (fonts, layouts, colors, mood), we can start on actually bringing your piece to life. This one's on me, though, so you can site back and relax for a bit.
